How To Use the Image Convertor

    1. Select image from the gallery

    2. Click on the lower section to choose format

    3. choose your format in which you want to convert

    4. Select here GrayScale, PNG, JPEG, WEBP, pdf for your image

    5. Then, Click on Submit button

    6. Once the success bar enable click on here to see the file

    7. Converted file will be shown in new tab
